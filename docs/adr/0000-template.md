## Context

What problem are we solving? What forces are at play — technical constraints, requirements,
prior decisions, alternatives considered? State the situation neutrally so a future reader
understands *why* a decision was needed, not just what we chose.

## Decision

The change we are making, stated as a clear, active assertion ("We will …"). Be specific:
name the components, interfaces, and behaviors involved.

## Consequences

What becomes easier and what becomes harder as a result. Include positive outcomes,
trade-offs, new obligations (e.g. things that must now be maintained), and follow-up work.
